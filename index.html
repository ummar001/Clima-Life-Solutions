Our Story

Founded with a vision to combat environmental waste, Clima Life Solutions specializes in refurbishing and reselling commercial refrigeration equipment. Our mission is to extend product life cycles, reduce landfill contributions, and make sustainable cooling accessible to all.

Our dedicated team combines technical expertise with a passion for the circular economy. We work tirelessly to ensure every unit meets the highest standards of quality and efficiency — preventing valuable refrigeration equipment from entering landfills by refurbishing, repurposing, and reintroducing assets back into the market.

We aim to build strong partnerships with businesses and communities to reduce waste and extend equipment life cycles — creating a cleaner, greener future, one fridge at a time.

Greener Cooling Starts Here

Assessment

At Clima Life Solutions, every refrigeration and air-conditioning asset we collect undergoes a structured assessment to determine its most sustainable next step. We evaluate the unit’s age, service history, and overall usage, alongside a detailed inspection of structural integrity, mechanical condition, compressor health, electrical components, and refrigerant compliance. Each asset is then reviewed for refurbishment feasibility through a cost-versus-value analysis to establish whether it can be professionally restored and reintroduced to the market. Where full refurbishment is not viable, we salvage reusable components and responsibly recycle remaining materials, ensuring maximum circular value and minimal waste at every stage.
